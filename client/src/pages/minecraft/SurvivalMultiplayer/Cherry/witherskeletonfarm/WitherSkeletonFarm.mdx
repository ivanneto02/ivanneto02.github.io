# Wither Skeleton Farm

Design: <a href="https://www.youtube.com/@ianxofour" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">ianxofour</a>

Similarly to the Iron Farm, the Wither Skeleton farm is a must-have endgame farm that allows me to build endless beacons for cheap. All it requires is plenty of iron for hoppers and a bit of ingenuity to link nether portals together and make mobs fall into them.

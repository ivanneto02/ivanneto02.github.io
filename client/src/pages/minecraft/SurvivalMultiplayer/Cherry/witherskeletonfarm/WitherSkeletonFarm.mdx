# Wither Skeleton Farm

Design: <a href="https://www.youtube.com/@ianxofour" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">ianxofour</a>

Similarly to the Iron Farm, the Wither Skeleton farm is a must-have endgame farm that allows me to build endless beacons for cheap. All it requires is plenty of iron for hoppers and a bit of ingenuity to link nether portals together and make mobs fall into them.

## How it works

This farm follows ianxofour's careful design. A nether fortress within a soul sand valley biome is found, then one of the nodes is turned into a spawn platform. Finally, we spawn-proof the platform with stone walls, and place turtle eggs in every corner. This will draw out zombified piglins. In the middle of the platform, we carefully place a cage with a golem to draw in wither skeletons and blazes. Since we will be waiting far above this spawn platform, the blazes do not attack the golems but simply move towards the nether portal.

At the kill chamber, a second nether portal is placed for the mobs to fall into. Over in the overworld, there is a transport platform to move the mobs from one nether portal to the other, thereby spawning them at the kill chamber. The kill chamber is made out of glass to prevent ghasts spawning.

We used trapdoors because they look kind of like windows. We used blocks and trapdoor togethers since in Japanese houses this is the style of look they go for. Overall, it turned out really well and we will reuse this style in future builds. The style in japanese in called <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shoji" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Shoji</a>. These are used throughout Southeast Asia such as in China, Japanese, among others.

# Tree Farm

Design: <a href="https://www.youtube.com/@ilmango" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Ilmango</a>.

Throughout our journey on this world, wood has always been an issue. Every farm that we make requires a storage unit, which needs to accomodate a ton of items. This requires plenty of wood to make chests, hoppers, and other miscellaneous items. Having a tree farm is essential to boost the production of farms, and our wealth within the world.

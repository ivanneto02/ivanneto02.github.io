# Nether Highway

Connecting the many projects we have worked on is the Nether highway. In Minecraft, the Nether is approximately 64 times smaller in area than the Overworld. This means that the length and width of the Nether world are 8 times smaller than the Overworld.

Traveling through the Nether is the superior travel mode, as it by default ensures that the player moves through the world 8 times faster than with normal running. Couple that with ice running and the Nether highway makes for the most optimal and resource-cheap method for travel.

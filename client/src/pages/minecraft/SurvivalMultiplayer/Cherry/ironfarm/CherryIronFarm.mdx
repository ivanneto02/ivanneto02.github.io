# Iron Farm

Design: <a href="https://www.youtube.com/@ianxofour" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">ianxofour</a>

This is a farm that I make on almost every survival world, whether singleplayer or multiplayer. Without iron, most of the projects I make cannot be done due to their high iron demand. For instance, I needed several stacks of hoppers to make my gold farm on <a href="ivanneto.dev/server/valley/goldfarm">Valley</a>. Thanks to ianxofour, this farm is easily accessible to every player who wishes for quick and easy iron ✔️.

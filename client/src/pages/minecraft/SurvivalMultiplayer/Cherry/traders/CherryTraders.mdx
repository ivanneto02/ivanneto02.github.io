# Village Traders

Trading with villagers is how we got most of the wealth in this world. We are able to obtain common items like several building blocks, or even utility items like paper, bookshelves, armor, and tools. Even more, by leveraging RNG we are able to obtain powerful enchanted books like Protection 5 and Sharpness 5, among others.

Overall, this trading post has made it possible for us to enchant all of our tools, and not have to worry about losing them. We are quickly able to make brand new tools, after all.
